Game Guide!
Here's a helpful video for if you need to review connect four.
  • "Let's Start a Game!" starts a game of connect four. If you are unsure of how to play, instructions can be explained from the video here.
  • "Settings" allows you to make in-game changes such as colors and board size. Click on the given button to cycle through the given options.
  • "Leaderboards" will let players see who has the most of a given stat. You have to login to have your stats tracked as you play.
  • "Log In or Sign Up" lets you register or login to your account. If you're logged in, the page will display your stats and provide an option to logout.
  • If you'd like to leave to the main menu at any point, click on the back arrow below. It'll be found at the bottom of all pages.
